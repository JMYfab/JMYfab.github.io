Raise money the easy way with a custom online apparel store. No inventory, no collecting money, no stress — we handle everything.

How It Works

1. We Build Your Store

We design and set up your custom online store with approved apparel, logos, and pricing.

2. You Share the Link

Share the store link with parents, players, staff, or supporters. Orders are placed and paid for online.

3. We Produce & Deliver

Once the store closes, we decorate all items and distribute them for pickup or group delivery.

4. You Get Paid

After production, we send your organization its fundraiser profit. Simple and transparent.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the store stay open?

Most stores run for 10–14 days, but we can adjust based on your needs.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum for the group — each supporter orders individually.

How are fundraiser profits calculated?

Each item includes a built-in fundraiser margin. After the store closes and production is complete, we send a payout summary and check.

What products can we offer?

We offer embroidered polos, hoodies, hats, tees, jackets, and more. We can also include promotional merchandise such as water bottles, key chains, and other engraved items.
